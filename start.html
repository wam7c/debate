The Digital Debate Assistant provides Challenger and Defender with a graphical way to envision aspects of a typical Tibetan monastic debate. Pages include a display of the possibilities for interaction between phenomena and a method for seeing the three modes of a correct syllogistic reasoning. Another page assists in setting-up and flinging of an absurd consequence that flows from an incorrect pervasion statement.
