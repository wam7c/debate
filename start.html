Open-Source Geshe, A Digital Debate Assistant, provides students of Tibetan logic with a graphical way to envision aspects of a typical Collected Topics debate. Pages include a display of the possibilities for interaction between phenomena and a method for learning the three modes of a correct syllogistm. Open-Source Geshe also assists in setting-up and flinging an absurd consequence flowing from an incorrect pervasion statement.
