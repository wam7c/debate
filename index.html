Open-Source Geshe: A Digital Debate Assistant

This website is designed to assist students of Tibetan logic and debate. Currently it has the following features:

  • Comparison of Phenomena

  • Pervasion Statements

  • Three Modes of a Correct Reason

  • Flinging Absurd Consequences

  • Four Possibilities

  • Mutual Inclusion

  • Mutual Exclusion

Under construction, August, 2019. William Magee, Maitripa College and the UMA Institute for Tibetan Studies.
